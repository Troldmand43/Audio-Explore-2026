# Audio-Explore-2026
The Audio Explorers 2026 case competition

You now have access to 5 engineering challenges with a different engineering focus:

Embedded Software
Software
Mechanical
Electrical
Diagnostics, DSP
The cases are all inspired by real engineering challenges within hearing healthcare and Diagnostics.

All relevant materials for solving the cases can be found here: Audio Explorers 2026 https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1sNkqT8HMJVq3tOeey4dHsP2-Te9GG4dH

You and your team are free to choose one or more challenges to solve. Whether you prefer to submit a report, a slideshow, a poster, an app prototype, or even a CAD drawing – it’s up to you unless the case states otherwise. The most important is, that you showcase your ideas, creativity and technical insights.
